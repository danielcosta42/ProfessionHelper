## 📋 Screenshot Best Practices

### Technical Requirements
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 or higher
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File size:** Under 5MB per image
- **Quality:** High (no compression artifacts)

### Composition Tips
- 🎨 Use default UI scale for consistency
- 🌙 Dark backgrounds work best
- 📏 Center the addon window
- 🧹 Close other UI elements (bags, etc.)
- ✨ Clean chat log (no spam)
- 🎯 Show meaningful data (not empty states)

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Player names (privacy)
- ❌ Guild chat/whispers
- ❌ Other addons cluttering the screen
- ❌ Low resolution or blurry images
- ❌ Dark/hard to see screenshots
- ❌ Empty/placeholder data
